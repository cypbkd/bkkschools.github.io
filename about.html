About

I'm a father of two, my older daughter is about 5 so I started house hunting to find a good school. The school ratings on the real estate website are not comprehensive, so I built this table to cover more ratings from all popular ratings websites including greatschools, schooldigger and niche.

Most of the records are manually added over time as I research houses. Not all schools are covered as they may not in my research area. Please feel free to submit missing records, it will help others as well.

If you have any suggestions or any feedbacks, please contact me.
